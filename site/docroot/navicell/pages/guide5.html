Who are investigators of NaviCell?

Term Explanation
Curator Explorers, critically analyzes and comments content of maps in the blog.
Map manager Creator of map(s) that uploads maps to NaviCell, maintains maps by collecting curators comments and updating maps accordingly.
System administrator Installs and maintains NaviCell web-application.
User Explores existing collection of the maps.
